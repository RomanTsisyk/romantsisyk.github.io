About Productivity Booster

Productivity Booster is a Chrome extension that enhances your productivity by structuring your work sessions and rest periods using the Pomodoro technique. Whether you're a professional, a student, or anyone looking to maximize their productivity, Productivity Booster is the perfect tool for you.

Key Features

  • Pomodoro Timer: Automatically structures your time into 25-minute work sessions followed by 5-minute breaks, ensuring focus and rest.
  • Long Breaks: After four work sessions, enjoy a longer 15-minute break to refresh and recharge.
  • Custom Timers: Customize your work sessions and breaks according to your personal rhythm.
  • Notifications: Receive alerts when it's time to start working or take a break.
  • Website Blocking: Stay distraction-free by blocking access to time-wasting websites during work sessions.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Easy to navigate and adjust settings for a comfortable user experience.

Why Choose Productivity Booster?

Productivity Booster offers a balanced work-rest routine, promoting better focus and preventing burnout. By eliminating online distractions, it helps create a productive work environment that encourages higher output. Embrace the power of the Pomodoro technique and experience a significant improvement in your productivity.
