About Me

Welcome! I'm an award-winning Android developer with 7+ years of experience building high-quality mobile applications for startups and enterprise clients worldwide. My journey in mobile development started in 2016, and I've been crafting exceptional digital experiences ever since.

I specialize in creating native Android applications using Kotlin and Java, with a strong focus on modern architecture patterns like MVVM, Clean Architecture, and the latest Android Jetpack components. My apps are built with performance, scalability, and engaging user experiences at their core.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me sharing knowledge at tech conferences, contributing to open-source projects, or exploring emerging technologies to stay at the cutting edge of mobile development.
