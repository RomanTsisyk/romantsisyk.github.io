Sport Apps :

My first start-up.

 it's a suite of free Android apps that use mobile technology to create personalised daily training plans for users. These apps are designed to help users achieve their fitness goals by providing tailored workouts and exercises based on their specific needs and preferences. The workouts include a variety of exercises, such as sit-ups, planks, push-ups, pull-ups, fat-burning workouts, training for legs and chest, dips, and squats. 

In addition to the Android apps, I also planning to create a web page that will allow users to access their training results and progress on any device. This will give users even more flexibility and convenience, as they will be able to check their progress from their computer or phone at any time. 

The apps feature a user-friendly interface that makes it easy for users to track their progress and see the results of their training. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced athlete, our apps and web page are designed to help you reach your fitness goals and improve your overall health and wellness. Try them out today and see the difference they can make in your training routine.
