Follow MyMoney

Follow MyMoney is an investment app designed for quality-conscious customers who want to make the most of their assets. Introducing the first multibank platform that covers all the major areas of successful financial investment.

Invest in a wide range of assets such as equities, ETFs, warrants, certificates, and cryptocurrencies with Follow MyMoney. Enjoy top-notch conditions while you manage your investments or follow the recommendations of selected and tested stock-market experts to benefit in real-time from their performance. Follow MyTrader takes social trading to the next level: Star Trading. The combination of our offerings sets us apart.

Successfully different. Follow our Stars to grow your wealth!

ALL POSSIBILITIES IN YOUR HANDS. SIMPLE AND SECURE: BROKER AND CRYPTO TRADING. ● Highest security standards, two-way verification ● Global stock-exchange trading, monitored price quality ● Wide selection of asset classes: equities, ETFs, certificates, warrants, cryptocurrencies ● German banking partners for highly secure crypto trading ● Trade crypto at competitive prices, spread from 0.25%, no commission ● No high-risk products such as CFDs ● Headquartered and regulated in Germany

MYTRADER. ● Follow Star Traders in real-time with just a click ● Don't miss gains with our auto-follow function ● Proven successful track record ● Transparency through trade explanations by Star Traders and insight into all trades and statistics.

OUR HIGHLIGHT: THE AUTOMATIC BUY/SELL FUNCTION. Whether you're in a meeting or on a plane, relax and let your Star Trader handle the perfect timing for a trade automatically. Never miss a buy or sell signal again. You decide whether to use this approach or execute each trade on your own initiative.

TRUST SELECTED INVESTMENT PROFESSIONALS. Each of our traders holds our Star qualification, meeting specific selection criteria: ● Proven trading success ● Consistent compliance with their own rules and strategy ● Strict risk management ● Independence from banks and corporate groups ● Personal assets used for every trade
