Lens App

Discover how Poly Lens unlocks a world of possibilities for your favorite Poly Bluetooth® devices. With Poly Lens, you can personalize your audio experience, stay up-to-date, and get assistance whenever you need it.

Poly Lens allows you to make the right adjustments to maximize your device's capabilities. Poly audio devices offer incredible acoustic features that enhance your experience, whether you're taking your phone to work or relaxing with your favorite music.

• Update your device with the latest software • Customize device settings to match your work style • Access helpful technical support • Locate your device with the Find My Device feature

IT administrators in enterprises interested in managing large-scale deployments can use the Poly Lens administrative portal to manage Bluetooth device policies, gain real-time insights into Bluetooth device data and usage across the office, and remotely troubleshoot issues from the cloud portal.
