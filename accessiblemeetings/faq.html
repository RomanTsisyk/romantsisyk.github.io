Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about AccessibleMeetings.

General Questions

What is AccessibleMeetings? +

AccessibleMeetings is a Chrome extension that provides real-time transcription for online meetings on Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and Zoom. It's designed to make meetings more accessible, especially for people with hearing impairments. The extension transcribes speech into text in real-time and displays it in an easy-to-read format.

Is AccessibleMeetings really free? +

Yes! AccessibleMeetings is 100% free to use with all features available to everyone. There are no premium tiers, no hidden costs, and no in-app purchases. We believe in making online meetings accessible to everyone without barriers.

Do I need to create an account to use AccessibleMeetings? +

No, you don't need to create an account or register to use AccessibleMeetings. Simply install the extension from the Chrome Web Store, and it's ready to use immediately. We designed it to be as frictionless as possible.

Which meeting platforms are supported? +

AccessibleMeetings currently supports:

  • Google Meet
  • Microsoft Teams
  • Zoom (web version)

We're working on adding support for more platforms in the future.

Installation & Setup

How do I install AccessibleMeetings? +

Installing AccessibleMeetings is simple:

  1. Visit the AccessibleMeetings page on the Chrome Web Store
  2. Click the "Add to Chrome" button
  3. Review the permissions and click "Add extension"
  4. The AccessibleMeetings icon will appear in your browser toolbar

For more detailed instructions, visit our Help Center.

What are the system requirements? +

AccessibleMeetings requires:

  • Google Chrome browser (version 89 or later)
  • Working microphone
  • Permissions to access the microphone in your browser
Why does AccessibleMeetings need microphone access? +

AccessibleMeetings requires microphone access to capture audio from your meeting and convert it to text using Chrome's built-in speech recognition. The extension only processes audio while you're actively transcribing a meeting.

All speech processing happens locally in your browser - no audio is sent to any servers.

Usage & Features

How do I start transcribing a meeting? +

To start transcribing:

  1. Join your meeting on Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, or Zoom
  2. Click the AccessibleMeetings icon in your browser toolbar
  3. Click "Start Transcribing"
  4. Allow microphone access when prompted (first time only)
  5. The transcription window will appear on your meeting page
What languages are supported? +

AccessibleMeetings uses Chrome's built-in speech recognition, which supports multiple languages. The exact languages available depend on your Chrome browser's language capabilities.

To change the language:

  1. Click the AccessibleMeetings icon
  2. Select "Settings"
  3. Choose your preferred language from the dropdown menu
How do I save a transcript? +

To save a transcript:

  1. After or during your meeting, click the "Save Transcript" button in the transcription window
  2. Choose where to save the text file on your computer
  3. The complete transcript will be saved as a text file

You can also click "Copy" to copy the entire transcript to your clipboard.

Can I customize the appearance of the transcription? +

Yes, AccessibleMeetings offers several customization options:

  • Text Size: Small (16px), Medium (18px), Large (22px), or Extra Large (26px)
  • Display Mode: Normal (black text on white), Dark Mode (white text on dark gray), or High Contrast (yellow text on black)
  • Window Size: Small, Medium, Large, or Fullscreen
  • Auto-scroll: Toggle to automatically scroll to show the latest text
  • Show timestamps: Toggle to include time with each entry

Access these settings from the extension popup before starting transcription.

Privacy & Security

How is my privacy protected when using AccessibleMeetings? +

We take privacy very seriously:

  • All speech processing happens in your browser
  • No audio or transcripts are sent to any servers
  • Transcripts are only saved locally when you choose to download them
  • We don't collect any personal data or meeting content
  • No account creation or login required
Can others see that I'm using AccessibleMeetings? +

The transcription window is only visible on your screen, not to other meeting participants. However, if you're sharing your screen, others will be able to see the transcription window if it's visible in the shared area.

Troubleshooting

The extension isn't working on my meeting platform +

If the extension isn't working:

  • Make sure you're on a supported site (Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, or Zoom web version)
  • Check that you're using a supported Chrome version (89 or later)
  • Try disabling other extensions that might interfere
  • Refresh the page and try again
  • Make sure you've granted microphone permissions

If problems persist, try reinstalling the extension.

The transcription quality is poor +

To improve transcription quality:

  • Use a high-quality microphone when possible
  • Reduce background noise
  • Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
  • Position yourself closer to the microphone
  • Make sure you've selected the correct language in the settings

Remember that speech recognition technology isn't perfect and may struggle with accents, technical terminology, or noisy environments.

My microphone isn't being detected +

If your microphone isn't being detected:

  • Make sure you've granted microphone permissions in Chrome (click the lock icon in your address bar)
  • Check that no other applications are using your microphone
  • Verify your microphone is connected and working properly
  • Try selecting a different microphone if you have multiple options
  • Restart your browser
